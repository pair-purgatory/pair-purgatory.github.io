# Pair Purgatory: Episode 1

## Brainstorming overview

- lizz gets chris to date brittany
- chris and brittany's date goes well
- lizz congratulates but is suddenly suspicious
- chris and brittany hang out again
- lizz thinks brittany kidnapped chris
- lizz saves chris and gaslights him into thinking brittany kidnapped him
  - chris hates brittany
- brittany is left confused
  - brittany still loves chris
  - brittany hates everyone protecting him
  - the serial problem begins:
    - brittany steals chris occasionally and puts him in situations to cope with her unconscious boundless love for him
    - the problem could be fixed if:
      - lizz had trust in brittany
      - brittany could realize her cope

## Chapters

### dream intro

- chris is left alone in a cold dark place and cries
  - symolizes his mental state when left alone
- his heart 'beats forward' and he follows it
  - symbolizes him following his heart without question hoping it will save him
- his heart leads him into someones arms
  - symbolizes trusting his heart with whoever it gets him with
- realizes its brittany and panics
  - symbolizes his trust in a random partner screwing him over
- lizz wakes him up

### chapter 1

- lizz realizes chris had _another_ nightmare about brittany again
- she tells him this could be fixed if we had trust in brittany
- she tells him to ask her out, he reluctantly accepts

### chapter 2

- lizz and chris are in the car
- chris panics but lizz reassures him
- lizz drops him off at brittany's
- he knocks on the door and brittany nervously greets him
- chris apologizes for everything and asks her out and she, out of nervousness, says no and shuts the door on him
- chris feels rejected and walks back to the car
- lizz consoles him on the way back

### chapter 3

- chris is playing minecraft and lizz backseat games for him
- mail falls through a slot in the door
- lizz checks it out and reluctantly gives it to chris without peering into it
- chris checks it out and its a letter from brittany
  - "did you mean everything you said? if u did come back 2 my place so i can apologize"
- chris tells lizz what it says and she takes him back there

### chapter 4

- chris is at brittany's door again
